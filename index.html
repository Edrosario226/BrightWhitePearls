Frequently Asked Questions

During a Pro White treatment, peroxide expands the oxygen pores on your enamel, allowing pesky surface stains to be oxidized quickly and painlessly. The result is a profoundly whiter smile. Most of our clients experience between 3 and 10 shades of whitening with one treatment. Additional treatments may be recommended, depending on the severity and origination of your stains.

For most people, the results are long lasting. Exposure to coffees, teas, tobacco, red wines and other stain-producing substances will eventually darken teeth over time. Age, genetics, and generalhygiene also have an effect on your treatments longevity.
